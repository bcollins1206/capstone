Gospel of Mark

Behold, I stand at the door, and knock: if any man hear my voice, and open the door, I will come into him, and will sup with him, and he with me... Revelation 3:20

Date and Place written: Between AD 57-63

Author: John Mark

Statistics: 41st book of the bible; 16 chapters; 678 verses; 15,176 words

The ultimate purpose for this site is to encourage believers and unbelievers alike to read God's word. Every word that comes from God is life and truth and will never come back void and we should be eager to hang on to every one. We have started with the book of Mark in the King James version and hope to introduce other versions in the future.

2 Timothy 3:16

16 All scripture is given by inspiration of God, and is profitable for doctrine, for reproof, for correction, for instruction in righteousness.17 That the man of God may be perfect, throughly furnished unto all good works.

And what better way to start than to learn of His living Word!

John 1:1

1 In the beginning was the Word, and the Word was with God, and the Word was God.

If you are reading this and you do not know Jesus Christ, that Living Word, my prayer for you is that He will reveal Himself to you as you seek Him through His word.

The book of Mark is divided up into sixteen chapters. We have displayed two chapters per page. On each page we have provided some bible trivia to make it fun and to test your knowledge. As you will notice as you go along the questions will get harder. At the end of the book we have provided a full trivia test over the whole book to help you in the understanding of Christ's ministry. Hope you enjoy!!!
